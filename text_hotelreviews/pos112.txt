architecturally the building is terrific you can feel the history of the place which just isn t there with purpose built chain hotels the arena has the look and feel of a boutique hotel lushly decorated wrought iron work on staircases chandeliers the up lighting and high windows and ceilings gives a great sense of space location is fab in the green space of oosterpark but only a ten min walk to the city halls the rooms are quirky with a small footprint the ground level has a closet small sofa wc and shower fridge table and chair with the huge bed on a mezzanine level there s a tv on each level
