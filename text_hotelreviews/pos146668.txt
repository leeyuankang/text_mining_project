marble arch by montcalm boutique hotel is a gem of a hotel situated about meters or so from the marble arch entrance to hyde park and equally close to the marble arch tube station it is within easy walking distance to the famous oxford and baker street and a short uber hop to all of the more famous landmarks of london we stayed at the marble arch london for just over a week and were extremely happy with the hotel from the check in we were upgraded at no extra cost to the breakfasts to the overall friendliness of the staff the rooms were more than adequate considering this is london and space is a premium we were not cramped at all
