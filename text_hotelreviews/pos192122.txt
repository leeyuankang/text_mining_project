this is my second time staying the weekend at tsuba with my partner we ve stayed at several other star parisian hotels in this price range and this is the best the staff are very welcoming and helpful the decor of the hotel is modern and very well done the rooms are a good size and the beds are incredibly comfortable the bathrooms with a tub are a good size the rooms are well furnished and about sq m which is great for paris the sauna hammam was amazing and clean the whole hotel is serenity an easy escape from the masses but still centrally located
