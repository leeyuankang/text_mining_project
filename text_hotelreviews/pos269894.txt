located in the innere stadt near to the opera house and really near to everything the museums are minutes walk away and there are restaurants everywhere the hotel was initially a little hard to find but o k after that it has an entrance on the pedestrian only annagasse and another one on krugerstrasse and it s a tall narrow building this hotel has a luxurious feel to it our room was large by european standards and decorated in the old style with a tiled bathroom according to the original design by gianni versace robes and slippers were soft and comfortable air conditioning was very good and wi fi in our room was good breakfast is included in the room rate you have to order from a menu the night before and can elect to have it in your room or served at your table in the cafe area although there are specified breakfast packages you can choose one as a baseline then add whatever you want from the others staff was friendly and helpful we would definitely stay there again
