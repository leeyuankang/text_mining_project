this four star hotel is conveniently situated just a few minutes stroll from the beach promenade local restaurants including a minute walk into the town centre past the zoo a very welcoming entrance with friendly staff happy to help any way they can large comfortable rooms with expansive bathrooms the only down side is that it is expensive to eat and park within the hotel as it s per day for parking and each for breakfast per day good facilities within the room including coffee and tea and free wifi there is also a lovely pool terrace bar and gym though we didnt use these overall a pleasant stay if you have the budget
