i have been to milan multiple times staying at other star hotels and this was my first time at the palazzo parigi great breakfast selection and charming winter garden definitely recommend breakfast downstairs large spacious rooms in light colors and great finishing giving a charming comfortable feel would definitely recommend the hotel for long stays couples shoppers families business travelers very welcoming staff throughout the hotel breakfast dinner room service reception concierge and porter and attention to detail is unparalleled walking distance to via monte napoleone and via della spiga duomo and galleria vittorio emanuelle are a short stroll away stay was impeccable and would definitely recommend the hotel great value for your money
