avenida palace was a beautiful hotel with an excellent location our room was large and overlooked the main boulevard fun to watch all the activity on the boulevard below just around the corner was a great walking street with many restaurants and outdoor cafes also some good shopping it s only a block away from the plaza catalunya where we took the hop on hop off bus a good value and very close to the ramblas the staff quickly called taxis when we needed them and were very friendly there s also a lovely rooftop terrace with degree views and a small pool wish we could have stayed longer
