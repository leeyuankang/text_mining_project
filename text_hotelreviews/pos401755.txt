we stayed in the rook s nest which was quirky and fun the bathroom is integrated into the bedroom very romantic the double storey room is very spacious and well appointed the hotel staff and facilities are excellent the room service meals were of very good quality and the breakfast al fresco is a lovely touch farringdon station and tube stop minutes walk down the road make for very convenient travel options there are also a number of really good eating establishments in the immediate vicinity interesting historical snippets pertaining to the area are to found around the common areas in the hotel
