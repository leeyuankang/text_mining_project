hotel manzoni is classy and elegant with lovely hardwood floors in the rooms we were delighted that our room was ready at am when we arrived and our deluxe room with a balcony at the front of the hotel was splendid it is within walking distance of the duomo and the vittorio emanuele galleria as well as the metro stop montenapoleone the restaurant is excellent and the staff were very helpful in giving directions to a grocery market and in confirming our early morning transportation to milan linate airport we highly recommend hotel manzoni
